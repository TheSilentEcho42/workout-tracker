## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. **Sign Up**: Click "Sign Up" on the home page
2. **Enter Your Information**:
   - Email address
   - Password (at least 8 characters)
   - Full name
3. **Verify Your Email**: Check your inbox for a verification email and click the link
4. **Sign In**: Return to the app and sign in with your credentials

### Guest Mode

Want to try the app without creating an account? Click "Continue as Guest" to:
- Try all features with sample data
- Create and track workouts
- Generate AI workout plans

**Note**: Guest data is temporary and will be cleared when you sign out. Create an account to save your progress permanently.

### First Steps

After signing in, you'll see your **Dashboard**. This is your fitness command center where you can:
- View your workout statistics
- See your current workout streak
- Access your recent workouts
- Start a new workout

## Creating Your First AI Workout Plan

The AI Workout Tracker uses advanced AI to create personalized workout plans based on your goals, experience, and preferences.

### Step 1: Navigate to AI Workout

1. Click **"AI Workout"** in the navigation menu
2. You'll see the workout plan questionnaire

### Step 2: Fill Out the Questionnaire

The questionnaire helps the AI understand your fitness profile:

#### Experience Level
- **Beginner**: 0-6 months of training experience
- **Intermediate**: 6 months - 2 years
- **Advanced**: 2+ years

#### Fitness Goals
Select all that apply:
- Build muscle
- Lose weight
- Improve strength
- Increase endurance
- General fitness

#### Training Schedule
- **Days per week**: How many days can you commit to training?
- **Time per session**: How long are your typical workouts?
- **Unavailable days**: Which days of the week can't you train?

#### Equipment Available
Select what you have access to:
- Home gym
- Commercial gym
- Bodyweight only
- Resistance bands
- Dumbbells
- Barbell
- Machines

#### Additional Information
- **Age**: Helps tailor intensity recommendations
- **Current fitness level**: Self-assessment of your current condition
- **Injury history**: Any injuries or limitations to consider

### Step 3: Generate Your Plan

1. Click **"Generate Workout Plan"**
2. The AI will create a personalized plan based on your responses
3. Review the generated plan options
4. Select the plan that best fits your goals

### Step 4: Save Your Plan

Once you've selected a plan:
1. Click **"Save Plan"** to add it to your saved workout plans
2. Access it anytime from the **"Workout Plans"** page
3. Start workouts directly from your saved plan

## Starting and Tracking a Workout

### Starting a New Workout

#### Option 1: Start from Scratch

1. Go to **Dashboard** or **Workout** page
2. Click **"Start New Workout"**
3. Enter:
   - Workout name (e.g., "Push Day", "Leg Day")
   - Optional description
4. Click **"Start Workout"**

#### Option 2: Start from a Saved Plan

1. Go to **"Workout Plans"** page
2. Click on a saved plan
3. Click **"Start Workout"** on a specific day
4. The exercises from your plan will be pre-loaded

### Adding Exercises

1. Click **"Add Exercise"** button
2. Search for an exercise or browse by muscle group
3. Select the exercise
4. Enter workout details:
   - **Sets**: Number of sets you plan to do
   - **Reps**: Target number of repetitions
   - **Weight**: Weight in lbs/kg (optional for bodyweight exercises)
   - **RIR (Reps in Reserve)**: How many reps you could do after completing the set
     - 0 = Maximum effort (couldn't do another rep)
     - 1-2 = Very hard
     - 3-4 = Moderate difficulty
     - 5+ = Easy
5. Click **"Add Exercise"**

### Tracking Sets During Your Workout

As you complete each set:

1. **Update Set Values**:
   - Click on a set to edit
   - Update weight, reps, or RIR based on what you actually did
   - The app saves automatically

2. **Add More Sets**:
   - Click **"Add Set"** if you want to do more sets than planned
   - Useful when you're feeling strong and want to push harder

3. **Remove Sets**:
   - Click the **"Remove"** button if you skip a set
   - No problem - adjust as needed

### Completing Your Workout

When you're done:

1. Click **"Complete Workout"**
2. Optionally add a workout summary:
   - How did it feel?
   - What went well?
   - What could be improved?
3. Enter workout duration (in minutes)
4. Click **"Finish Workout"**

Your workout is now saved to your history!

## Tips for Effective Tracking

### 1. Be Consistent

- Track every workout, even if it's shorter than planned
- Consistency in tracking helps you see real progress over time

### 2. Use RIR (Reps in Reserve) Accurately

RIR helps track training intensity:
- **0 RIR**: Maximum effort - use sparingly
- **1-2 RIR**: Ideal for strength building
- **3-4 RIR**: Good for volume and muscle building
- **5+ RIR**: Too easy - consider increasing weight

### 3. Track Everything

Don't skip tracking:
- Bodyweight exercises (use 0 or leave weight empty)
- Warm-up sets (optional, but helpful for reference)
- Failed sets (still valuable data)

### 4. Review Your History Regularly

- Check your progress weekly
- Identify patterns (e.g., "I always struggle on Mondays")
- Celebrate improvements (new PRs, increased volume)

### 5. Use AI Summaries

After completing workouts, the AI can generate:
- **Strengths**: What you did well
- **Improvements**: Areas to focus on
- **Next Steps**: Recommendations for future workouts

### 6. Plan Ahead

- Save workout plans for your routine
- Plan your week in advance
- Adjust plans based on how you feel

### 7. Stay Honest

- Record actual performance, not what you planned
- If you did 8 reps instead of 10, record 8
- Accurate data leads to better insights

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes! All data is encrypted and stored securely. Only you can access your workout data.

**Q: Can I use the app offline?**
A: Offline support is coming soon! For now, you need an internet connection to save workouts.

**Q: Can I export my workout data?**
A: Export functionality is planned for a future update.

**Q: Is there a mobile app?**
A: The web app works great on mobile browsers. Native mobile apps are planned for the future.

### Workout Tracking

**Q: What if I forget to track a workout?**
A: You can manually add past workouts, but it's best to track in real-time for accuracy.

**Q: Can I edit completed workouts?**
A: Yes! Go to History, click on a workout, and you can edit sets and details.

**Q: What's the difference between "planned" and "in_progress" workouts?**
A: 
- **Planned**: Workout scheduled but not started
- **In_progress**: Currently tracking a workout
- **Completed**: Finished workout saved to history

**Q: How do I delete a workout?**
A: Go to History, click on the workout, and use the delete option.

### AI Workout Plans

**Q: How accurate are AI-generated plans?**
A: The AI uses advanced algorithms trained on fitness best practices. However, always listen to your body and consult a healthcare provider for medical concerns.

**Q: Can I modify an AI-generated plan?**
A: Yes! You can edit exercises, sets, and reps in any saved plan.

**Q: How much does AI plan generation cost?**
A: The app uses cost-effective AI models. Basic plans are very affordable, and complex plans may have slightly higher costs (typically less than $0.10 per plan).

**Q: Can I create my own workout plans?**
A: Yes! You can create custom workouts by adding exercises manually, or modify AI-generated plans to fit your preferences.

### Exercise Tracking

**Q: What if I can't find an exercise?**
A: You can create custom exercises! Go to the exercise search and click "Create Custom Exercise."

**Q: How do I track bodyweight exercises?**
A: Leave the weight field empty or enter 0. The app will still track reps and sets.

**Q: What's RIR and why is it important?**
A: RIR (Reps in Reserve) measures training intensity. It helps you:
- Track how hard you're working
- Plan progressive overload
- Avoid overtraining
- Optimize recovery

**Q: Can I track cardio or time-based exercises?**
A: Yes! For exercises like running or planks, use the duration field instead of reps.

### Progress and Analytics

A: Total weight = sum of (weight Ã— reps) for all sets. This gives you total volume moved.

**Q: Why don't I see progress on some exercises?**
A: Make sure you're:
- Using consistent exercise names
- Tracking regularly
- Completing workouts (not just starting them)

**Q: Can I compare progress across different time periods?**
A: Progress comparison features are planned for future updates.

### Account and Settings

**Q: Can I change my email or password?**
A: Go to your Profile page to update account information.

**Q: What happens if I delete my account?**
A: All your workout data will be permanently deleted. This action cannot be undone.

**Q: Can I have multiple accounts?**
A: Yes, you can create multiple accounts with different email addresses.

### Troubleshooting

**Q: My workouts aren't saving.**
A: 
- Check your internet connection
- Make sure you're signed in
- Try refreshing the page
- Check browser console for errors

**Q: The app is slow or not loading.**
A:
- Clear your browser cache
- Try a different browser
- Check your internet connection
- Disable browser extensions that might interfere

**Q: I'm getting error messages.**
A:
- Take a screenshot of the error
- Check if you're signed in
- Try signing out and back in
- Contact support with error details
